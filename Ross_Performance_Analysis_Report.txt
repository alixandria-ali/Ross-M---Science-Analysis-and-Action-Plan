DETAILED RECOMMENDATIONS TO REACH 90% ON NEXT TEST
================================================================================


HEAT & THERMAL ENERGY (STUDY PLAN)
------------------------------------------------------------
- Learn that heat ALWAYS flows from hot to cold (never the reverse)
- Understand that objects absorb thermal energy from warmer surroundings
- Study how temperature difference affects the rate of heat transfer
- Review evaporative cooling: evaporation removes thermal energy
- Practice identifying the direction of heat flow in various scenarios


NEWTON'S LAWS / FORCE & MOTION (STUDY PLAN)
------------------------------------------------------------
- Review Newton's Three Laws of Motion thoroughly
- Practice problems involving F=ma (Force = mass x acceleration)
- Understand that greater mass = less acceleration when force is constant
- Study Newton's Third Law: forces always occur in equal and opposite pairs
- Work on problems comparing objects with different masses


ELECTROMAGNETIC SPECTRUM & WAVES (STUDY PLAN)
------------------------------------------------------------
- Memorize the EM spectrum order: Radio > Microwave > Infrared > Visible > Ultraviolet > X-ray > Gamma
- Remember: Radio waves have LONGEST wavelength, Gamma rays have SHORTEST
- Understand amplitude-energy relationship: higher amplitude = more energy
- Study properties of different types of waves and their uses


LIGHT INTERACTIONS (STUDY PLAN)
------------------------------------------------------------
- Clearly distinguish between four light behaviors:
  * REFLECTION: light bounces off surfaces (mirrors)
  * TRANSMISSION: light passes through materials (clear glass)
  * ABSORPTION: light is taken in by materials (dark surfaces)
  * REFRACTION: light bends when entering different materials
- Practice identifying which process occurs in different scenarios


EARTH SCIENCE (ORBITS/SEASONS) (STUDY PLAN)
------------------------------------------------------------
- Study Earth's tilt and how it causes seasons
- Learn which hemisphere has summer/winter at different orbital positions
- Understand moon phases: half the Moon facing Sun is always illuminated
- Practice comparing planetary distances and orbital relationships
- Review scale and proportional reasoning with astronomical distances


GENETICS & DNA (STUDY PLAN)
------------------------------------------------------------
- Review DNA > RNA > Protein (central dogma of biology)
- Understand that mutations in DNA can change protein structure/function
- Learn genotype notation: AA or aa = homozygous, Aa = heterozygous
- Study how genetic changes affect organisms


ELECTRIC CHARGES (STUDY PLAN)
------------------------------------------------------------
- Master the fundamental rule: LIKE charges REPEL, OPPOSITE charges ATTRACT
- Remember: same charge (both positive or both negative) = repulsion
- Study how objects gain charge through friction (rubbing)
