## 🎯 How to Use These Lessons

### For Each Lesson:

1. **Watch the videos first** (if you prefer visual learning)
2. **Read the lesson carefully** - don't skip sections
3. **Pay special attention to "Common Mistakes"** - these are based on your actual test patterns
4. **Complete ALL practice questions** - don't just read them
5. **Check your answers** and read ALL explanations (even for questions you got right)
6. **Review the Quick Reference Card** - great for last-minute review
7. **Complete the Self-Check Quiz** - make sure you can say YES to everything

### Study Tips:

- ✅ **Active recall:** Cover answers and try to solve first
- ✅ **Spaced repetition:** Review topics multiple times over days
- ✅ **Teach someone:** Explain concepts to a friend/parent
- ✅ **Make flashcards:** Use Quick Reference Cards
- ✅ **Practice, don't just read:** Work through all practice questions
- ✅ **Understand, don't memorize:** Focus on WHY, not just WHAT
