## 🔑 Key Facts to Master

**Quick Reference Card:**
- Heat flows HOT → COLD (never cold to hot)
- F = ma (more mass = less acceleration with same force)
- Newton's 3rd: Equal and opposite forces ALWAYS
- EM Spectrum: Radio (longest) > Gamma (shortest)
- LIKE charges REPEL, OPPOSITE charges ATTRACT
- Mirror = REFLECTION | Clear glass = TRANSMISSION
- Higher amplitude = MORE energy
- Evaporation ABSORBS thermal energy (cooling effect)

## 💡 Study Techniques That Work

1. **Active Recall** - Cover notes and explain concepts out loud
2. **Practice Problems** - Work through problems, don't just read solutions
3. **Concept Mapping** - Draw diagrams and create comparison charts
4. **Error Analysis** - Review each question in CSV and understand WHY
5. **Timed Practice** - Simulate real test conditions
